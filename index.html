How To Use

MetCon Artist generates CrossFit workouts based on different structures and modalities.

Workout Structures:

  • Single: One modality (Weightlifting, Gymnastics, or Monostructural)
  • Couplet: Two modalities combined
  • Triplet: Three modalities combined
  • Chipper: Multiple movements in sequence

Example Workouts:

  • Single Weightlifting: Clean & Jerk EMOM for 10 minutes
  • Couplet: Deadlifts + Pull-ups for time
  • Triplet: Snatch + Handstand Push-ups + Row AMRAP
